Indoor Volleyball vs. Beach Volleyball

There are several differences between beach volleyball and indoor volleyball

  1. Beach Volleyball is palyed in sand and indoor volleyball is played in a gymnasium.
  2. There are different sized courts. The beach volleyball court is smaller than an indoor volleyball court. A beach volleyball court is 16m x 8m. An indoor volleyball court is 18m x 9m.
  3. Different types of balls are used. Beach volleyballs are softer, lighter, and a tab bit bigger. Indoor volleyballs are made of leather and are heavier
  4. In beach volleyball, there are only 2 players on each side of the court. In indoor volleyball, there are 6 players on each side of the court
  5. Because there are only 2 players in beach volleyball, there are no specific positions, However, players must know all of the skills: hit, pass, and block. In indoor volleyball, there are each player has a specfic position.
